Uncle John's Come to the House

The Miller Family: Father, Mother, Laura (10), Karen (8), Bob (5).

Chapter 1: Surprise Visitor

One day 5-year-old Bob was playing in the sand-box, suddenly he heard a car coming, Bob quickly ran into the house calling "Mother! There's someone in a red car." Soon Mother walked down the stairs. Suddenly they heard, knock, knock, knock.

Chapter 2: Uncle John's come to visit

"O hello, John," Mother said, opening the door. "Hello Lucy," said Uncle John, "we were passing by your house and decided we would visit. Is your Father here?" Uncle John asked, as Mother hustled to get supper ready. "No, he is still at work."

Chapter 3: Uncle John's stay for the night

Soon Father came home and by then Uncle John's whole family was in the house. John's family was his wife Martha, & his children 16-year-old Lucy, 14-year-old Benjamin, 10-year-old Beth, 8-year-old Susan, 7-year-old James and 6-month-old Heidi.

Chapter 4: Uncle John's leave

After supper, Bob and James began to play. The next morning Uncle John's had to leave.
